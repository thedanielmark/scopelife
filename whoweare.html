WHO WE ARE

We’re a group of philanthropists made up of renowned community leaders, doctors and a social engineer, working under the active leadership of Mr. Parthipa and Mrs. Prameela as Founders. The founder Mr. Parthipa, himself is a victim of stigma, as he hails from Leprosy affected family and that has led to the creation of ‘SCOPE’! The ‘SCOPE’ has re-emerged to challenge the community-social behavior with several innovative initiatives which are essential to mitigate stigma and ensure social justice under the new leadership of Dr. Arup K. a Social Engineer and an Author, who plays a key role in raising & training children, youth and women as ‘Ambassadors of Peace & Holistic Health Management’ who play a crucial role at the grass-root levels as preventive measures against several communicable diseases and social injustices.

The ‘SCOPE’ is governed by a group of youth leaders as a team, raised and nurtured with suitable skills, who too were once victims of circumstances and are deeply rooted in the community’s environment, socio-cultural life, passionate and understand the local context better. These youth leaders are entrusted to identify challenges, take up pre-emptive steps, educate and empower and positively influence the citizens towards creating an ‘enabling condition’ for the citizens to come together and bring in the much desired changes in social behaviours which would sustain development at every local context, ensure social & economic benefits reaching to the most needy, and develop resilient communities to end several disasters causing endless sufferings to both the people & planet …towards achieving at least 6 of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals of UN.

VISION

Developing a ‘just society regardless of religion and ethnicity’ devoid of illiteracy, ill-health, inter-generational poverty, exploitation, child marriage and gender based violence especially against children and women that deter communities from thriving and a peaceful living;

SIX PILLARS OF WOMEN’S PARLIAMENT AUTHORITY NETWORK’ – (WPAN)

SIX PILLARS OF HOLISTIC HEALTH MANAGEMENT – (HHM) THROUGH WOMEN’S PARLIAMENT’

MISSION

The three primary missions of ‘SEEDS’ are to:

-Lay the ‘Seeds of Functional Education’ especially to the hard-to-reach girls and women from most backward communities, making them fully employable, self-worth and instrumental to bring their communities out of inter-generational poverty;

- Restore ‘Community Health and raise the Ambassadors for Holistic Health Management’ – (AHHM) by forming children, youth and women’s parliaments (CPAN) in their villages & schools enabling them to develop resilient communities; and

- Remove ‘Structural In-equalities of Women’ (education, reproductive health, employments), ending exploitations and violence against them towards developing the gender-just thriving communities with an enabling condition, while working with community institutes and leaders.
